Partner With Us

We would love to work with other like-minded organizations.

Specifically we are interested in partnering with organisations/Self-Help Groups that can:

  • Improve IT infrastructure at our centres with respect to laptops, PCs, netbooks and internet technologies
  • Provide expertise as regards curriculum development
  • Source volunteers for our centres and help expand to new centres
  • Assist us in opening up new centres

We also welcome

  • CSR groups
  • College social service clubs
  • Educational trusts who can provide scholarships and financial assistance to our students

Current Partners

Current Deepam Partnerships & Supporting Organizations include:
