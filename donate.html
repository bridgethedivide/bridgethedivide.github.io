Donate - Money, books and laptops

Donate money: You are welcome to donate any amount you wish to, based on your interest and capacity. Your donation will help a young organization like ours go the distance in helping children get access to Computers and other facilities.

Donations to Deepam Charitable Trust are eligible for exemption under Section 80G of Income Tax Act, 1961.

Our needs are specifically:

  1. Infrastructure to teach computers: Power Supply, Laptops, Internet access at centres
  2. Curriculum Material: Reading and writing material, Books and dictionaries for the children

Deepam needs funds to run its centres and expand to new projects. Currently, we are supported by individual donors.
