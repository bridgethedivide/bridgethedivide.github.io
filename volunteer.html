Volunteer with us

Deepam is a volunteer driven organization. We reach out to 240 kids across 5 centers in Chennai every weekend - thanks to people like you who wish to make a difference.

How do you start?

The best way to start off is to visit one of our centers during a class (Saturdays). What are you waiting for? Give it a shot and come down for a 'few' trial classes, interact with the kids and see how we work!

Induction Program

We conduct Induction Programs at regular intervals to welcome our new volunteers, and inform them about Deepam, how we go about our teaching and mentoring, and responsibilities and roles of volunteers.
